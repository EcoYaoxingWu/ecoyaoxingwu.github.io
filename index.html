About Me

I am a Ph.D. student specializing in soil microbial ecology and global change ecology. My research explores plant-soil-microbe interactions under climate change, with emphasis on biodiversity conservation and ecosystem restoration. I integrate large-scale ecological patterns with microbial mechanisms to understand how soil communities respond to and shape environmental changes, contributing to sustainable ecosystem management.
